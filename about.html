About Me


Introduction

I am a Diploma in Library Informatics or CDIM144 student of class 4F under UiTM Cawangan Kedah, passionate about books and coffee. You can call me IRA! "Life is about making an impact, not making an income." Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

Background

My full name is Nurul Syakirah binti Asril and I was born in Kuala Lumpur on the 10th of December 2004. I came from a family of six and is the eldest out of four siblings. My mother runs a bakery, so I have more or less experienced the life of a bakery owner's daughter. It is to the point that I can only stomach my mother's desserts (everything else is simply not on par, heh). It may seem cliche, but being the eldest child and having a businesswoman as my mother have shaped my professional journey and personal growth all these years, and I am eternally grateful for the life I have been blessed with.

Hobbies and Interests

In my free time, I love to read and play games, as I find the most joy and relaxation in these activities. For example, I have started reading a specific genre of books since June of 2022, and exactly two years later, I have read a total of 60 books of that genre!

Philosophy and Goals

My personal philosophy is to always entertain my curiosity and flirt my way into it. This belief has been guiding me in my daily life and motivates me to strive for many things in life, like strengthening my faith and religion. I'm committed to continuous learning and growth, and I aim to make my parents the proudest they will ever be for as long as they exist.
